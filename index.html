Tax Tracker

A simple service to designed to simplify
data aggregation for tax purposes.

Tool Info

Swapping like crazy? No biggie. I got you. For a fee of 20A, I will compile a history of all transactions associated your alogrand wallet address. This data can then be used to determine things like your cost basis, capital gain/loss associated with a given swap pair, gain/loss from NFT sales, or gain/loss from LP.

What you'll get

You will receive your compiled transaction history in two forms; csvs and excel workbooks. You will get one csv for each swap pair (e.g. ALGO/STKE, ALGO/DEGEN), NFT transaction (AGA197/ALGO), and single transactions (e.g. receiving claimed staking rewards from algostake for BIRDS, directly send or receiving ALGO or any ASA). The excel workbooks will be separated by swaps, NFTs, and single transactions. Each workbook will contain a separate spreadsheet for the each pair (see example image below). The main advantage of the excel worksbooks is that they can be viewed in a GUI like Microsoft Excel (paid) or Google Sheets (free).
